About Us

The Rookies are a band from Dublin, Ireland. Founded in 2015, they began as a 4 piece band consisting of Vocals, Guitar, Bass, and Drums. Since then their sound has evolved along with the band members. Currently the band consists of brothers Daniel and Eoin Maher, sharing vocals and guitar, Donogh Lane on bass, and Anne-Marie Farrely on lead guitar.
